Gameplay & Statistics Data

We collect comprehensive gameplay data to provide core game functionality:

Player Statistics

  • Game Performance: Wins, losses, win rate, games played
  • Skill Metrics: APM (actions per minute), reaction times, accuracy
  • Progression: Experience points, levels, achievements unlocked
  • Competitive Data: Rankings, tournament performance, win streaks
  • Time Tracking: Session duration, total playtime, peak activity periods

Real-Time Gameplay Actions

  • Game Commands: All actions taken during matches (attacks, building, movement)
  • Territory Control: Land ownership, resource management, expansion patterns
  • Alliance Activities: Alliance requests, diplomatic relations, trade actions
  • Strategic Decisions: Unit deployment, tactical choices, timing patterns

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Universal Rights (Available to All Players)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Communication Preferences: Opt-out of marketing communications

Enhanced Rights (EU/EEA, UK, California Residents)

  • Opt-Out of Processing: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data in specific circumstances
  • Automated Decision-Making: Opt-out of automated profiling or decision-making
  • Data Protection Authority: File complaints with your local privacy regulator

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sovereignio@gmail.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days and may need to verify your identity to protect your privacy.

9. Children's Privacy

Age Requirement: 13+ Only

Sovereign Lines is intended for players aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

COPPA Compliance (United States)

  • We require age verification during account creation
  • Players must confirm they are 13 or older to create accounts
  • If we discover a user is under 13, we will immediately delete their account and data
  • Parents can contact us to inquire about their child's potential account

International Standards

  • EU/GDPR: Users aged 13-16 may need parental consent depending on their country
  • Additional Protections: Enhanced privacy settings for users under 18
  • Parental Rights: Parents can request information about their minor child's account
